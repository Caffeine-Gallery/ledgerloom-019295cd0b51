Frequently Asked Questions

What is ICRC-2?

ICRC-2 is a token standard for the Internet Computer, providing a consistent interface for fungible tokens.

Tokens can be acquired through various means, including exchanges, airdrops, or participation in the project's ecosystem.

What can I do with these tokens?

These tokens can be used for various purposes within the ecosystem, including governance, transactions, and accessing specific features.

Yes, the total supply is fixed and can be viewed in the token information section above.
